I am a tinkerer at heart. Woodworking, soldering, and even embroidery - I love working with my hands. My experience as a fabrication and embedded systems intern at Tsalla Aerospace, coupled with three years as a student worker at Haverford College’s MakerSpace, has honed my skills in fast prototyping. Whether it be rebuilding a crashed drone in time for a test flight or helping a fellow student design CAD models for their latest project, I’ve learned to approach challenges with a mindset rooted in a willingness to explore, fail, and iterate.
