Rotating Lamp

This rotating lamp was designed to explore the intersection of functionality and aesthetics. The project involved designing a complex gear train in Fusion 360, 3D printing the components, and assembling them to create a dynamic and visually appealing lamp. The rotating mechanism is powered by a stepper motor, and the lamp provides both ambient lighting and an engaging motion feature.
