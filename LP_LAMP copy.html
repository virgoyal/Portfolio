Rotating Little Prince Night Lamp

The Little Prince is one of my favorite books, and I wanted to honor its timeless charm by creating a night lamp inspired by its whimsical art and profound themes. This lamp is a labor of love that combines art, engineering, and storytelling.

This project presented a unique design challenge: the lamp's LEDs, are powered by a wall socket. If the LEDs were connected to the drum, the rotation would cause the wires to twist, tangle, and eventually break. To overcome this I designed a small platform (on which we would mount the LEDs) that would stay stationary inside the drum and a gear train that would facilitate the rotation of the drum around the shaft of this platform. I fed wires through this 3D printed mechanism from the base, that housed all the electronics, to the drum. Here is a clip of me designing it in Fusion 360 to better illustrate what I mean -->

The lamp features engraved illustrations from The Little Prince, etched by a laser-cutter into wooden panels. Each side tells a unique story, with elements like the fox, the rose, and the stars bringing the magic of the book to life.
